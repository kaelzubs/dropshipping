While we strive to meet estimated delivery times, delays may occur due to customs, local postal services, or unforeseen supplier issues. We are not liable for delays outside our control.

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you receive a defective or damaged item, you may request a return within {{ return_window_days }} days of delivery.
